What is CLWS?


CLWS (Credit Liability Weighted Score) is a credit score system that allow previously loan-illegible applicants to apply for loans. We create our scores based on your crebit liabilites, that include outstanding payments on non-official liabilites like insurance premiums. Through this system, we aim to cater to a large populace who got turned down loans because of a low credit score.

How to maintain a good CLWS?


CLWS is not a complex system and depends mainly on your repayment habit, hence, making timely payments on your outstanding debts.
